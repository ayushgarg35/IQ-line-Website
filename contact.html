FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?
Implementation timelines vary by scope. A single-module deployment such as LIMS typically takes 2 to 4 weeks. A full HIMS deployment with all modules typically takes 8 to 16 weeks.
Do you support government hospital procurement processes?
Yes. IQ-Line supports GeM, state tender processes, and direct procurement by government health departments, along with the documentation required to move procurement smoothly.
What kind of ongoing support do you provide?
We provide ongoing technical support through phone, email, and remote channels. Critical implementations can also include on-site support and regular system review cycles.
Is data stored on-premises or on the cloud?
IQ-Line supports both deployment models. Public institutions often choose on-premises or NIC Cloud environments, while private operators may prefer managed cloud deployments.
Can IQ-Line integrate with our existing systems?
Yes. IQ-Line is designed for interoperability and supports integration patterns for hospital systems, analyzers, PACS, billing, and ABDM-aligned exchange.
What is your pricing model?
Pricing is typically based on institution type, scale, module mix, and implementation complexity. Reach out with your context and we can suggest a realistic starting plan.
