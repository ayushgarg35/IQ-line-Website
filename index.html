Why IQ-Line?

Healthcare Technology That Actually Works in India

We don't copy Western SaaS models. IQ-Line is engineered from the ground up for Bharat — addressing real constraints like low bandwidth, power outages, multilingual staff, and complex public healthcare workflows.

  • Offline-First Architecture

    Systems continue to work during internet outages — critical for district hospitals & rural PHCs.

  • Multilingual Support

    Supports 22+ Indian languages — Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Telugu and more for staff & patients.

    HL7 FHIR, ABDM-compliant, integrates with NHA, CoWIN, and Ayushman Bharat ecosystem.

    Early Warning Systems, diagnostic AI, and predictive analytics built right in.
