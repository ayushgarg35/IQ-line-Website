How EWS Works

1
Vitals Entry Nurses record vitals on tablet/mobile at bedside — or auto-pulled from bedside monitors.
2
AI Scoring Engine NEWS2, SOFA, qSOFA scores calculated instantly. ML model predicts 6-hour deterioration risk.
3
Tiered Alerts Low/Medium/High/Critical alerts escalate automatically — nurse → doctor → senior doctor.
4
Response Tracking Every alert response is logged for audit, quality improvement, and clinical governance.
