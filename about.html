Compliances & Certifications

Built on a Foundation of Trust & Compliance

IQ-Line products are designed from the ground up to meet India's healthcare regulatory requirements — compliance is never an afterthought.

NABH Ready

Our HIMS and modules support NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals) accreditation requirements — clinical documentation, infection control records, and quality indicators are all tracked.

ABDM / ABHA Integration

Full compliance with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM). Patients' ABHA IDs are linked, health records shared securely via the health locker, and PHR apps integrated.

Ayushman Bharat / PMJAY

Our billing module supports Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) and state insurance scheme claim processing, pre-authorisation, and settlement with TPA integration.

HL7 FHIR R4

All IQ-Line products are built on HL7 FHIR R4 — the international standard for healthcare data exchange — ensuring interoperability with any modern healthcare system.

DPDP Act Compliance

Fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 — data minimisation, consent management, and purpose limitation built into all data handling workflows.

NMC / MCI (Medical Education)

MUMS and SMS products are fully aligned with National Medical Commission (NMC) regulations — CBME curriculum, attendance requirements, and annual reporting automated.

NACO Compliance (Blood Bank)

BMS is compliant with National AIDS Control Organisation (NACO) guidelines for blood bank operations, including mandatory testing protocols and reporting requirements.

NIC Cloud / MeghRaj

IQ-Line products can be deployed on NIC Cloud (MeghRaj) and GI Cloud infrastructure as required by government institutions for data sovereignty compliance.
